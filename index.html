Why choose Coders Studio?

Coders Studio is the premier STEM-based academy in Delhi, established in August 2019 with a vision to create an online platform dedicated to providing exceptional Coding, Robotics, and STEM education to children aged 4 to 15 years. We are dedicated to making STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, & Math) education both enjoyable and relevant for students, fostering a passion for these subjects from an early age. 

Our programs develop essential 21st-century skills such as creativity, teamwork, logical thinking, critical thinking, analytical skills, and empathy. Our unique “hands-on learning”  pedagogy emphasizes active learning by engaging both the mind and the hands. This approach ensures that children learn best through participation, making education both effective and enjoyable.

What is STEM Education?

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) education is a window to understanding the building blocks of life, the universe and everything. It gives us tools to explore, question, navigate in, and even invent the world we live in – skills that can and will be applied in every field of work and play. 

For most of us, STEM fields are either wildly exciting and inspiring or extremely daunting concepts that may be difficult to relate to. They often get a reputation as the “hard” sciences. Hard, both as in difficult to grasp and learn, but also with a reputation of being rigid, leaving very little, if any, room for creativity or imagination.
